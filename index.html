Description
Drops is an exhibitory website exploring the consumption of analog culture by digital means, the works that have lost value or place by this process, and the people and projects attempting to reverse these effects.
The Death of Flash ☠️
First released in 1993 by FutureWave Software, Flash was adopted by web developers and creatives as a (mostly) universal platform for multimedia content. Large communities, such as Newgrounds and Albino Blacksheep, formed around the creation of Flash animation and games.
Flash fell out of favor for its poor performance and and security, and new technologies such as HTML5 took its place.
Preservation projects such as BlueMaxima's Flashpoint provide a secure sandbox to experience these works natively.
